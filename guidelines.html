Symptoms

  1. Fever or chills
  2. Cough
  3. Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing
  4. Fatigue
  5. Muscle or body aches
  6. Headache
  7. New loss of taste or smell
  8. Sore throat
  9. Congestion or runny nose
  10. Nausea or vomiting
  11. Diarrhea

Treatments

There is currently no specific antiviral treatment for COVID-19. However, people with COVID-19 can seek medical care to help relieve symptoms. If you have symptoms of COVID-19, contact your healthcare provider to find out if you should be tested and if you need medical care. In severe cases, treatment may include hospitalization, oxygen therapy, and other supportive care.

Guidelines

  1. Wear a mask in public settings and around people who don’t live in your household.
  2. Stay at least 6 feet away from others.
  3. Avoid crowded indoor spaces and ensure indoor spaces are properly ventilated.
  4. Wash your hands often with soap and water. Use hand sanitizer if soap and water aren’t available.
  5. Cover coughs and sneezes with a tissue or use the inside of your elbow.
  6. Clean and disinfect frequently touched surfaces daily.
  7. Monitor your health daily and be alert for symptoms.
  8. If you have symptoms of COVID-19, stay home and away from others.
  9. If you have been in close contact with someone who has COVID-19, get tested and stay home and away from others for 14 days.
