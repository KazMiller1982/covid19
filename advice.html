Advice regarding the disease and vaccine

It is important to follow the advice of public health officials to protect yourself and others from COVID-19. Here are some key things to keep in mind:

  1. Get vaccinated when a vaccine is available to you.
  2. Wear a mask in public settings and around people who don’t live in your household.
  3. Stay at least 6 feet away from others.
  4. Avoid crowded indoor spaces and ensure indoor spaces are properly ventilated.
  5. Wash your hands often with soap and water. Use hand sanitizer if soap and water aren’t available.
  6. Cover coughs and sneezes with a tissue or use the inside of your elbow.
  7. Clean and disinfect frequently touched surfaces daily.
  8. Monitor your health daily and be alert for symptoms.
  9. If you have symptoms of COVID-19, stay home and away from others.
  10. If you have been in close contact with someone who has COVID-19, get tested and stay home and away from others for 14 days.
  11. Stay informed about the latest developments regarding COVID-19 and follow advice from public health officials.
